# Genre Recipes for Circuit Tracks

Quick reference for genre-specific settings. Use these as starting points, not rules.

## Techno

- **BPM**: 125-140 (classic: 130)
- **Scale**: Minor, Dorian, or Chromatic
- **Synth1** (Bass): preset `bass`, saw/square wave, filter_frequency 40-70, short decay, mono mode
- **Synth2** (Lead/Stab): preset `lead` or `pluck`, short stab chords or arpeggiated lines
- **Drums**: kick on 1/5/9/13, closed hi-hat on every step or offbeat (2/6/10/14), clap/snare on 5/13, open hat on 9
- **FX**: subtle reverb on synths (send 20-40), delay on lead (send 30-50, time synced), sidechain synth1 from drum1
- **Pattern**: 16-32 steps, repetitive with subtle variation via p-locks on velocity/gate

## House

- **BPM**: 118-128 (classic: 124)
- **Scale**: Major, Minor, or Dorian
- **Synth1** (Pad/Chord): preset `pad`, warm chords, filter_frequency 80-100, long release
- **Synth2** (Bass): preset `bass`, round sub bass, filter_frequency 50-70
- **Drums**: four-on-the-floor kick (0/4/8/12), offbeat hi-hat (2/6/10/14), clap on 4/12, shaker on every step at low velocity
- **FX**: warm reverb (decay 70-90), delay on chords (time 64, feedback 40), light sidechain on pad from kick
- **Pattern**: 16 steps, groove from swing (55-60)

## Ambient

- **BPM**: 60-90
- **Scale**: Major, Melodic Minor, or Whole Tone
- **Synth1** (Pad): preset `pad`, slow attack (80+), long release (100+), filter_frequency 90-120, LFO on filter
- **Synth2** (Texture): preset `pad`, detuned osc2 (cents 60-68), noise_level 20-40, long everything
- **Drums**: sparse or none — if used, soft hits at low velocity (40-60), irregular placement
- **FX**: heavy reverb (decay 100+, send 60-80), long delay (feedback 80+, time 80), no sidechain
- **Pattern**: 32 steps, sparse notes with long gates (0.9-1.0)

## Drum & Bass

- **BPM**: 170-180 (classic: 174)
- **Scale**: Minor, Minor Pentatonic, or Blues
- **Synth1** (Bass): preset `bass`, aggressive saw, filter_frequency 30-60, short decay, mono with glide
- **Synth2** (Pad/Lead): preset `pad` or `lead`, atmospheric chords or reese-style layering
- **Drums**: breakbeat pattern — kick on 0/10, snare on 4/14, hi-hats fast (every 2 steps), ghost notes at low velocity
- **FX**: short reverb on snare, delay on lead, heavy sidechain on bass from kick
- **Pattern**: 32 steps essential for breakbeat feel

## Hip-Hop

- **BPM**: 80-100 (modern trap: 130-145 half-time)
- **Scale**: Minor Pentatonic, Blues, or Dorian
- **Synth1** (Bass/808): preset `bass`, sine/triangle wave, long decay, low filter, mono
- **Synth2** (Melody): preset `lead` or `pluck`, simple melodic hooks
- **Drums**: boom-bap kick on 0/6/8, snare on 4/12, hi-hat rolls with velocity variation, open hat accents
- **FX**: subtle reverb on snare (send 20-30), delay on melody (send 20-40)
- **Pattern**: 16 or 32 steps, swing 55-60 for groove

## Synthwave

- **BPM**: 100-120 (classic: 110)
- **Scale**: Minor, Dorian, or Harmonic Minor
- **Synth1** (Pad/Arp): preset `pad`, lush saw pads with chorus, or arpeggiated plucks
- **Synth2** (Bass): preset `bass`, pulsing saw bass, filter modulated by LFO
- **Drums**: electronic kicks and snares, gated reverb feel — kick on 0/8, snare on 4/12, toms for fills
- **FX**: lush reverb (decay 80-100, send 50-70), chorus on pads (depth 60-80), subtle delay
- **Pattern**: 16 steps, straight timing (swing 50)
